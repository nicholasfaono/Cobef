The COBEF is the main Brazilian congress of Manufacturing Engineering promoted every two years by the Brazilian Society of Engineering and Mechanical Sciences (ABCM). The purpose of this congress is to offer a wide forum for presentations and critical discussions of new results from fundamental research applied to the manufacturing technologies. The COBEF scope covers the following areas:

 

  • Casting
  • Machining
  • Metrology
  • Tribology
  • Metal Forming
  • Power Metallurgy
  • Virtual Manufacturing
  • Surface Engineering
  • Welding and Materials Union
  • New Manufacturing Technologies
  • Additive Material Processes
  • Non Metallic Materials and Composites processing
  • Design for Manufacturing and Assembly: Planning, Managing and Automation

 

In this year of 2015, COBEF is being organized by SENAI Bahia through its unit SENAI CIMATEC with the help of Brazilian Deep Drawing Research Group.
